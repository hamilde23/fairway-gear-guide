About Fairway Gear Guide

Our mission is to help golfers choose the right equipment and connect with brands.

Why We Exist

Golf equipment can be expensive and confusing. Fairway Gear Guide was created to provide clear, honest reviews of clubs, accessories and training aids so you can make informed decisions. We research the latest gear, test it on the course and use affiliate partnerships to fund our work.

Working With Brands

We start by reviewing products we already own or borrow from friends because real‑world experience builds trust. We also reach out to local pro shops and rental stores to see if they will loan us items in exchange for exposure. Once our audience grows, we pitch manufacturers and distributors directly, highlighting how our targeted community of golfers offers value beyond raw traffic numbers. When appropriate, we purchase or rent new gear to review and recoup those costs through affiliate commissions and advertising.

Get Involved

Are you a golf brand or PR representative looking to get your gear in front of passionate players? We’d love to hear from you. Contact us to discuss review opportunities, sponsorship packages or long‑term partnerships.
